I'm a developer, gamer, musician, bartender, designer, builder, backpacker, nomad, world citizen, traveler

Throughout the last 15 years in my career, I have worn many hats. I'm ready to emback on a fulfilling career as a software developer.

Full Stack Developer

I went to college, got a degree, got a career and bought a house. All the things I was supposed to do. I worked in Sales and made good money, but it didn't feel right. I was unfulfilled and tired everyday. So, I had to make a change.

Since I watched Star Trek TNG as a kid, I've always been fascinated by tech. I'm always reading articles about the latest tech breakthroughs. For the longest time, I envied those working in gaming, software, space, and engineering. So, after taking time to breathe and bartend, I decided to join the Iron Yard!

I tried to learn software development on my own for 3 years. I learned many languages, but had little application. The Iron Yard is a 12 week intense and immersive experience, requring 65+ hours a week of coding and application. It worked me into shape!

Now I wake up thinking about code and I'm addicted to solving complex issues. I also have side projects with Pleiades Design and Development, a group I helped build for my cohort to find side work and develop our own ideas. Check out some of our projects here -> Pleiades
